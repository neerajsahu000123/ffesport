Privacy Policy

// Last Updated: January 2025 · Esports Archive India

Summary: We collect minimal data, don't sell your information, use Google Analytics and AdSense (industry standard), and you can opt out any time.

1. Who We Are

Esports Archive ("we", "us", "our") is an independent fan-operated historical database for Indian Free Fire esports. We are not affiliated with Garena International or any esports organization.

Contact: admin@esportsarchive.in

2. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected:

  • IP address (anonymized, for analytics)
  • Browser type, device type, operating system
  • Pages visited, time on site, referral source
  • Country / region (not exact location)

Admin Users Only:

  • Username and password (hashed, stored server-side)
  • Session token (temporary, expires in 24 hours)

We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or payment information from regular visitors.

3. How We Use Information

  • Understand which content is most useful to visitors
  • Improve site performance and navigation
  • Show relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Diagnose technical issues

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects anonymized usage data.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google uses the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads
  • Third-party vendors may use cookies for interest-based advertising
  • You can opt out at: google.com/settings/ads
  • Or via: Network Advertising Initiative

We comply with the Google AdSense program policies and do not place ads on pages with inappropriate content.

6. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Session cookie: Temporary, for admin login only. Deleted when browser closes.
  • Google Analytics cookies: _ga, _gid, _gat — track usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Google AdSense cookies: For ad personalization (can be opted out)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note: disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share anonymized analytics data with:

  • Google (Analytics + AdSense) — for site analytics and ads

We may disclose information if required by Indian law or court order.

8. Data Storage & Security

Our site data is stored on secure cloud servers. Admin credentials are stored as hashed values. Session data is server-side and expires after 24 hours. We use HTTPS encryption for all connections.

9. Children's Privacy

Our site is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights (Indian Users)

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Know what data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics and advertising tracking

Contact us at admin@esportsarchive.in to exercise these rights.

11. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to external sites (YouTube, social media, esports organizations). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the updated version with a new date at the top. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions:

Website: esportsarchive.in
